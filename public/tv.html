Watch Notflix on your LG TV

Pick a method below. Start watching in seconds in your TV browser, or install it as a real app with a one-click installer.

On your LG TV, open the Web Browser app and go to this address. It loads straight into the big-screen layout — use your remote's arrows + OK.

mittamovies.netlify.app/?tv=1

Tip: bookmark it on the TV for one-tap access next time.

1On your TV: install the Developer Mode app from the LG Content Store, sign in with your LG account, then turn Dev Mode Status = ON and Key Server = ON.
Note the IP address and the 6-character passphrase it shows. You'll need Node.js installed on your computer.
2On your computer: enter your TV's IP and download your personal installer. It downloads the app, registers your TV, installs & launches Notflix — all in one go.
3Run the installer you just downloaded (double-click it on Windows, or bash install-notflix.sh on Mac/Linux). When it asks, type the passphrase from your TV. That's it — Notflix appears on your home screen. 🎉
Prefer to do it manually? (or just grab the app file)
⬇ Download notflix-webos.ipk

Then run (replace TV_IP):

npm install -g @webos-tools/cli
ares-setup-device --add notflixtv -i "host=TV_IP" -i "port=9922" -i "username=prisoner"
ares-novacom --device notflixtv --getkey
ares-install --device notflixtv notflix-webos.ipk
ares-launch --device notflixtv com.notflix.app
⏱️ Heads up: LG side-loaded apps pause after ~50 hours; the TV renews them automatically as long as the Developer Mode app stays installed and signed in. (LG limitation for all dev-mode apps.)
